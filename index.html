The Basics

For building a D&D class, there are some basic steps you need to follow. First, roll your stats using the stat generator down below. These will help you decide what strengths and weakenesses your character has.


Next, pick your character race & class. Be sure to check our Info Page to help you decide.


After choosing those, its time for your equipment and skills! We'll offer you some starting equipment options, as well as skill recommendations. Your class will also inherently give you proficency in certain skills.
